Behind the Production

Proses Produksi yang Terstruktur & Transparan

Setiap project kami jalankan melalui alur kerja yang jelas—untuk memastikan hasil akhir sesuai ekspektasi dan siap diproduksi secara konsisten.

Step 01

Discovery & Brief

We discuss your brand positioning, target audience, material preferences, and budget constraints. This shapes the entire production strategy.

⏱ 1-2 days 💬 Free consultation
Step 02

Design & Material Selection

Technical drawing, dieline setup, and material specification. We provide recommendations based on your product protection needs and brand aesthetics.

⏱ 3-5 days 📐 Technical spec included
Step 03

Physical Sample & Approval

Dummy sample creation for structure validation. This ensures correct dimensions, material feel, and assembly before full production begins.

⏱ 7-10 days 📦 Physical dummy provided
Step 04

Production & Quality Control

Full-scale manufacturing with in-line quality checks. Every box goes through inspection for print accuracy, structural integrity, and finishing quality.

⏱ 4-6 weeks ✓ Quality guaranteed
